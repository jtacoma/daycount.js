Backwards-incompatible changes:

* @day@ has been renamed to @moment@, supporting fluency across counts whose days begin and end at different times.
* @daycount.counts.thoth@ has been renamed to @daycount.counts.venus@.

Added support for counts:

* *Local Julian Day* (@daycount.counts.localJulianDay@)
* *Mercury Count* (@daycount.counts.mercury@)
